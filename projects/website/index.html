I created a website!

March 2020 · 1 min · Category: Computer Science

At the beginning of 2020, the CoronaVirus pandemia created a state of fear and illness all over the world. The national governments decided to set a lockdown for the whole population trying to limitate the spread of the virus. That’s how I ended up with free time I didn’t imagine to have and no possibility to go out of my home. I started to wonder…what if I did a website? My website?

So here it is, it all started like this.

The world is countinuously changing, and so are we. But even from bad moment (or maybe, mostly from them!) we can grow and create good things.
